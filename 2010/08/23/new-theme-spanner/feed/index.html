Yes, I have opinions, and communicate them via muttering to husband. Very effective.

My view on length:
– micfic is micro – we are meant to be struggling with squeezing it in under the word limit. I have learnt a lot from this about how little is necessary to tell a story, and how much of what I write has little power and can go! I’d like to leave it at 250 for the foreseeable future. Maybe review once per year
– Agree (and even own) stv’s point – I really want to (and try to) comment on each micfic. I so value all of your comments! Longer micfic will take more time to analyse and comment, and I’m topping out right now.
– I like the uniformity of length as we are all faced with the same challenge, but respond so differently – it’s fascinating!
– I have always liked the idea of letting people be as creative as they like as long as they post the 250 word story too. Post the 350 word version separately! Post a 2000 word version! Post a 500 word story that is a completely different take on the theme! I would like to create a category called “extra homework” (or something), and let the creative outlets flow that way.

that’s all my cents.

No, wait, @extemp and maybe for another discussion: re 280 words – I have always interpreted the 250 as “anywhere over 220 and under 290″ or something like that. I don’t see anything wrong with 280 and it is within totally acceptable parameters for me. Views from others?
